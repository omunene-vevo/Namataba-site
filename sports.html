WELCOME TO NAMATABA SENIOR SCHOOL

SPORT ACTIVITIES


Sports play a vital role in the holistic development of students, our schools provide a perfect platform for young athletes to explore their talents, learn valuable life skills, and build a strong foundation for their future. It also aids in maintaining a healthy weight and reducing the risk of various health conditions such as obesity, cardiovascular diseases, and diabetes. By encouraging sports in our schools, sports teachers promote a healthy lifestyle and instill habits that can last a lifetime.
